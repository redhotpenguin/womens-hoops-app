Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app ask for my location?
Only when you tap Watch Nearby on a game. Your location is used once, on-device, to search for sports bars near you using Apple Maps. We don't store or transmit it.
Why does the app ask for calendar access?
Only when you tap Add to Calendar on a game. The app adds a single event (date, venue, broadcasts) to your default calendar. It never reads existing events.
Do you collect any personal data?
No. The app has no analytics SDKs, no tracking, and no third-party advertising. Your favorite team is stored only on your device.
Where does the game data come from?
Schedule, standings, scores, and league leaders come from ESPN's public sports API. We don't run our own backend.
I added a reminder — when will it fire?
Local notifications fire one hour before the scheduled tip-off, in your device's local timezone. Notifications run entirely on your device and require no internet connection.
The widget isn't updating.
iOS refreshes Home Screen widgets on its own schedule (usually within an hour). You can force a refresh by removing the widget and adding it back.
How do I delete the app and all its data?
Long-press the app icon on your Home Screen and tap Remove App → Delete App. Because we don't sync to a server, deleting the app erases everything.
