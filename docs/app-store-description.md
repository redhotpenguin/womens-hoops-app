# App Store Description — Women's Hoops Tracker

Paste into **App Store Connect → (version) → Description**. Trademark-free per
the Guideline 4.1(a) fix (no league name, no "the W," widget named "Next Game").

---

Women's Hoops Tracker is the simplest way to follow women's pro basketball on your iPhone and iPad.

See the next two weeks of games at a glance — every matchup, tip-off time in your local timezone, venue, and where to watch online. Drill into any game for live scores, the broadcast list, and a one-tap reminder. Browse the standings, drill into a team's upcoming and recent schedule, and check the top players in points, rebounds, assists, steals, and blocks. Pick a favorite team to filter your schedule. Add the next-game widget to your Home Screen.

GAMES
  • Two weeks of upcoming games with date, tip-off time, and venue
  • Color-coded team chips for every matchup
  • Local timezone shown next to every game time
  • Tap a venue to open it in Apple Maps
  • Watch Online: tap any game's network list and head straight to the broadcaster
  • Watch Nearby: find the 5 closest sports bars showing the game (uses your location, on-device only)

GAME DETAIL
  • Live score updates every 30 seconds during in-progress games
  • Networks broadcasting the game with direct tap-to-watch links
  • "Remind me 1 hour before tip-off" — local notification, no account needed
  • "Add to Calendar" — drops the game into your default calendar with venue + broadcasts

STANDINGS
  • Western and Eastern conference standings, sorted by record
  • Rank, W, L, win percentage, and games behind for every team
  • Tap any team to see their upcoming and recent games

LEADERS
  • Top 10 across Points, Rebounds, Assists, Steals, and Blocks
  • Up-to-date for the current season

HOME SCREEN WIDGET
  • "Next Game" widget in small and medium sizes
  • Shows the matchup, date, tip-off time, and primary broadcaster
  • Refreshes automatically

SETTINGS
  • Pick a favorite team to filter the schedule to "My Team"
  • Share the app with a friend via AirDrop, Messages, or Mail
  • No accounts, no sign-ups, no ads

PRIVACY
  • No analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs
  • Location is used only when you tap Watch Nearby, and stays on-device
  • Calendar access is write-only — we never read your existing events
  • Your favorite team is stored only on your device

Schedule, standings, scores, and leader data are sourced from ESPN's public sports API.
